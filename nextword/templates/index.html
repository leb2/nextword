Usage

For when you want to find that next word that's just on the tip of your tongue. Simply type an asterisk "*" for the word that you are missing. For example, 
He was *
will return the words that come after the phrase "He was" You can also have multiple wildcards. For example: 
what * it *
Note: Max number of words is 6. Searches might be a bit slow since 2.6 million n-grams need to be counted for each search.
